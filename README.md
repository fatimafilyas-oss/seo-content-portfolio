## Content Writing Process

For each content sample, I follow this process:

```text
1. Understand the topic and target audience
2. Research the subject and competing content
3. Identify primary and secondary keywords
4. Prepare a clear article outline
5. Write content with proper headings and structure
6. Optimise readability and search relevance
7. Review and improve clarity before publishing
